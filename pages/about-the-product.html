About the Product

Nexa gives cold-chain B2B teams one operating view.

Nexa is designed for importers, distributors, commercial teams, logistics operators, and B2B buyers that need clearer control over refrigerated and frozen products. The AV2 product scope connects real backend-supported modules with explicit frontend demonstrations for planned modules.

AV2 scope
Catalog, inventory, orders, dispatch, invoices, payments, and buyer visibility are organized around cold-chain workflows.

Product model

What Nexa coordinates

The product is not a generic ecommerce surface. It models the operational work behind B2B cold-chain distribution.

01

Commercial flow

Catalog data, brands, categories, buyer requests, purchase orders, and credit/payment context help sales teams move from request intake to validated B2B orders.

02

Operational flow

Warehouses, inventory items, stock movements, shipments, route preparation, and delivery evidence support logistics teams responsible for refrigerated execution.

03

Buyer flow

The buyer portal gives B2B clients visibility into requests, orders, documents, payment references, and delivery progress without depending on scattered messages.
