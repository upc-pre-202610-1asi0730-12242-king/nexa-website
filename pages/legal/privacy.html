This page explains how Nexa handles operational data, local browser state and product flows in the academic evaluation environment.

01

What Nexa Is

Nexa is an academic B2B cold-chain platform. It presents connected product, inventory, dispatch, document and buyer-tracking workflows for evaluation.

02

What Data May Be Used for Evaluation

The evaluation environment may include sample names, companies, SKUs, buyer requests, dispatch states, document references and operational notes. These should be synthetic or academic examples.

03

Operational Workspace Data

The WebApp stores workspace records in a persistent tenant-scoped database. The academic environment is not a commercial production deployment and should not receive sensitive business data.

04

Local Browser Storage

The public site may use local browser state for language preferences or review session behavior. Clearing browser data may remove these local preferences.

05

Payment Method Previews

Payment-related interface elements are visual previews. Nexa v1 does not collect card details, process transactions or connect to a payment processor.

06

AI Assistant Preview

AI assistant behavior, when shown, is a prototype preview or simulated workflow. Nexa v1 does not provide a live AI service for operational decisions.

07

External Integrations Not Active in v1

GPS, IoT sensors, SUNAT, payment providers and external customer portals are not active production integrations in v1. They may appear only as roadmap or simulated scope.

08

User Rights and Contact

Anyone who has shared evaluation information with the team may ask what was received, request correction, or ask that those records be removed from academic materials where practical.

09

Data Deletion Request

If you want operational data removed or purged from a review environment, contact the academic team and identify the sample record or submission you want removed.
