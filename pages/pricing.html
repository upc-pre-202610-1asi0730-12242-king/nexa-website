    FAQ

    Pricing questions operators ask first.

    Nexa v1 is an academic frontend demo. It validates product direction and workflows, but it does not include a production backend or service commitment.

    Standard includes the simulated billing and payment demo: checkout, taxes, shipping summary, method selection and payment confirmation without real processing.

    Premium adds advanced catalog experience, customer-specific discounts, AI preview expansion, predictive demand recommendations and analytics exports.

    Real external customer portal integration, SUNAT or document-provider integration, GPS or IoT provider integration, SSO, custom APIs and dedicated infrastructure belong in Enterprise planning.
