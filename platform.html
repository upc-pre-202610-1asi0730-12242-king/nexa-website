02

Live Inventory

See what you have, where it is, and when it expires — across every warehouse and cold room. FEFO rotation is automatic: the system picks the lot closest to expiry first. No more stockouts discovered too late, no more expired product shipped by mistake.

03

B2B Orders

Your clients place orders through a portal where they see your real catalog, real prices, and real stock levels. No more phone calls to confirm availability. No more email trails. Orders come in structured and ready to dispatch.

04

Temperature & Delivery

Your drivers record temperature and collect a digital signature at every delivery stop — using their own phone. You get photo proof, GPS location, and exact handover temperature. When a client disputes a claim, you have the evidence.

05

Full Traceability

Every product has a complete record: where it came from, every warehouse it passed through, what temperature it was at, and who signed for it. Compliance documents are generated automatically — ready for audits whenever you need them.

How It Works

Your team doesn't need training to use this.

Nexa is designed for warehouse floors and delivery routes — not IT departments.

01

Managers use the dashboard

A browser-based dashboard that works on any laptop or tablet. See stock, orders, temperatures, and alerts in one view. No software to install.

02

Drivers use their phones

The mobile interface runs in any smartphone browser. Drivers record deliveries, take photos, and log temperatures. Works offline in low-connectivity areas — data syncs when signal returns.

03

Clients use the B2B portal

Your distributors and HORECA clients log in, see your catalog with live stock and prices, and place orders directly. You stop being the bottleneck between order and dispatch.

04

Alerts come to you

Temperature deviations, low stock, offline chambers — Nexa notifies the right person automatically. You don't need to watch a dashboard all day to catch problems.

No special hardware required. Nexa works with the devices your team already has. If you have temperature sensors, we connect to them. If you don't, we recommend compatible options during onboarding.
