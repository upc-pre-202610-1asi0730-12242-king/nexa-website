The Problem

Manual data entry creates operational dead weight.

Orders come through texts and emails, requiring manual transcription into ERPs. Stockouts are only discovered when picking the order. Drivers select whichever cheese or deli lot is closest to the dock door, not the lot that expires first. Dead stock piles up in the back of the chamber.

The Cost of Disconnect

  • Constant back-and-forth calls with restaurants and deli buyers
  • High rate of order inaccuracies
  • Revenue lost to spoilage due to poor rotation
