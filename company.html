We built Nexa because no system existed
for the way charcuterie and dairy operators actually work.

We spent months inside refrigerated-food operations — importers, distributors, cold storage facilities. The same problems kept appearing: cheese stock tracked in spreadsheets, charcuterie orders coming through WhatsApp, temperature data locked in local loggers that nobody checked until a food-safety audit or a claim.

These weren't careless operators. They were running serious businesses without a system designed for their reality. Generic ERPs didn't cover temperature. Logistics apps didn't cover inventory. Nobody connected the dots from catalog to delivery. So we built Nexa to do exactly that.

How We Support You

What happens after you say yes.

We don't hand you a login and disappear. Here's what working with us actually looks like.

A real person handles your onboarding

Not a tutorial and not a handoff email. Someone from our team maps your operation, loads your catalog, connects your sensors, and trains your people directly.

You talk to humans, not tickets

When something breaks or needs adjusting, you reach our team directly. No chatbot, no ticket queue, and no waiting days for a useful answer.

We stay for 30 days after go-live

We monitor your first real dispatches, confirm temperature data is syncing correctly, and check that FEFO is working in live operations before we step back.

Your data stays yours

You own your inventory data, your routes, your customer relationships, and your temperature logs. We never sell or share your information. You can export everything at any time.

Works with your existing hardware

We don't force you to buy new equipment. If you have sensors, we connect to them. If you don't, we recommend options that fit your budget and setup during onboarding.

Works offline

Drivers in areas with poor signal can still capture temperature, signatures, and GPS. Everything syncs automatically when the connection comes back.
