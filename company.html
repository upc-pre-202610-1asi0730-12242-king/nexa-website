About Nexa

Built by people who've worked
in cold-chain operations.

We're a small team in Lima. We build the operational system that cold-chain logistics has been missing — because we've seen what happens without one.

We built Nexa because no system existed
for the way cold-chain operators actually work.

We spent months inside cold-chain operations — importers, distributors, cold storage facilities. The same problems kept appearing: stock tracked in spreadsheets, orders coming through WhatsApp, temperature data locked in local loggers that nobody checked until an audit or a claim.

These weren't careless operators. They were running serious businesses without a system designed for their reality. Generic ERPs didn't cover temperature. Logistics apps didn't cover inventory. Nobody connected the dots from catalog to delivery. So we built Nexa to do exactly that.

How We Support You

What happens after you say yes.

We don't hand you a login and disappear. Here's what working with us actually looks like.

A real person handles your onboarding

Not a video tutorial. Not a knowledge base link. Someone from our team maps your operation, loads your catalog, connects your sensors, and trains your people — in person or via video, depending on what works for you.

You talk to humans, not tickets

When something breaks or needs adjusting, you reach our team directly — not a chatbot, not a queue. We respond within hours, not days. If it's urgent, you call us.

We stay for 30 days after go-live

We monitor your first real dispatches. We check that temperature data is syncing correctly, that FEFO rotation is working, and that your team feels confident. We don't leave until you're running smoothly.

Your data stays yours

You own your inventory data, your routes, your customer relationships, and your temperature logs. We never sell or share your information. You can export everything at any time.

Works with your existing hardware

We don't force you to buy new equipment. If you have sensors, we connect to them. If you don't, we recommend options that fit your budget and setup during onboarding.

Works offline

Drivers in areas with poor signal? The mobile interface continues recording temperature, signatures, and GPS location. Everything syncs automatically when connectivity returns.

Built by Operators

Nexa was founded in Lima, Perú by a team of engineers and supply chain operators. We saw firsthand the massive operational leakage that happens when cold-chain distribution relies on disconnected temperature logs, generic ERP tracking, and fragmented WhatsApp communication.

We don't sell visionary promises. We deploy hard operational fixes. When you work with us, you are supported directly by the execution team mapping your physical workflows into reliable digital infrastructure.
