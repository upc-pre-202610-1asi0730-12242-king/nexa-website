Getting Started

Nexa is the central operating system replacing manual spreadsheets and WhatsApp messages for charcuterie, cheese, and dairy workflows.

Instead of fragmented tools, Nexa gives your team a single, reliable screen to manage inventory, track temperature condition, and coordinate dispatch without guesswork.

Nexa is built for three types of operators:

Importers & Wholesalers — managing high-volume cheese, charcuterie, and dairy imports with container-level thermal monitoring and bulk inventory assignment.

Distributors — running last-mile delivery for specialty cheese, deli, and dairy orders with B2B portals, FEFO-enforced dispatch, and digital proof of delivery.

Cold storage operators — managing multi-chamber facilities, cheese aging rooms, and dairy stock with real-time monitoring, automated alerts, and food-safety logging.

If you manage charcuterie, cheese, dairy, or other refrigerated food at commercial scale, Nexa applies to your operation.

Nexa connects to SAP, Oracle, and any ERP with a published REST API. Data flows in both directions — you don't have to re-enter anything manually.

The Platform

Product Data — your full product list with SKU codes, temperature ranges, shelf-life windows, and pricing. Live across your distributor network.

Live Inventory — real-time stock levels across all nodes. FEFO rotation enforced automatically. Alerts when stock drops below thresholds.

B2B Portal — a self-service ordering interface for your distributors, retailers, and buying teams. They see available stock, place orders, and track dispatch status without calling you.

Mobile Telemetry — your drivers collect digital delivery signatures and temperature readings at handover. All evidence is logged and timestamped automatically.

Traceability — every lot movement from producer to final delivery, with full custody chain and thermal history. Food-safety documents generate automatically.

Yes. Modules can be activated progressively. Many operators start with catalog and live inventory, add the B2B portal once their distributor network is onboarded, and activate telemetry when they're ready to instrument their fleet.

The five modules share a single data layer, so adding a module doesn't require data migration or reconfiguration — the new module simply reads from what's already there.

When a B2B order comes in, Nexa automatically assigns stock from the lot with the nearest expiry date, not the newest lot. Your dispatch team doesn't decide — the system decides for them, based on the expiry data you entered when the lot arrived.

This means you stop selling older stock to whoever orders last. Every order draws from the correct lot automatically. No write-offs due to rotation errors.

Every lot receives a full chain-of-custody record: origin (producer, location, production date), transit handoffs (carrier, container, temperature at each transfer), storage (chamber assignment, FEFO lot number), and final delivery (digital signature, temperature at handover, GPS timestamp).

Compliance documents — in PDF format — are generated automatically from this record. They're available for download at any point and are formatted for standard refrigerated-food and food-safety audit requirements.

Your catalog and inventory visibility can be live within days of signing. The full implementation — including hardware sensor calibration, ERP integration, and team training — runs over a 12-week structured timeline.

The 12-week timeline is not "waiting" — it's active deployment. Your team is operational on the core modules within the first two weeks. The remaining time covers integration testing, edge-case configuration, and 30-day supported operation.

Nexa is hardware-agnostic. It integrates with any temperature sensor or monitoring device that provides a data output — including Onset, Sensitech, Controlant, and any brand with a REST or MQTT interface.

You don't need to replace existing hardware. If your sensors already log data, we connect to that data feed. If you don't have sensors yet, we recommend compatible options as part of the onboarding audit.

Security & Data

You do. Your inventory data, your customer relationships, your order history, and your temperature logs are owned by your organization. Nexa stores and processes them to power the platform — we never sell, resell, or share your data with third parties.

You can export your full data set at any time in standard formats. If you stop using Nexa, your data leaves with you.

Your data is backed up constantly and kept entirely separate from other clients. Everything is encrypted so that only your authorized managers can view your inventory or customer order histories.

For technical and compliance teams: we meet continuous refrigerated-food audit requirements through immutable 7-year audit logs. Our cloud infrastructure is triple-redundant, meaning a localized physical server failure does not interrupt your dispatch capabilities.

Nexa operates with a 99.9% uptime SLA. The platform is built for graceful degradation — if a network failure occurs, the mobile telemetry module continues logging data locally and syncs when connectivity is restored.

Critical operations (like driver delivery confirmation) continue to function offline. The dashboard will display cached data and clearly indicate any sync delay.
